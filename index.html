About BEC

Basaveshwar Engineering College (BEC), Bagalkote is an autonomous institution approved by AICTE and affiliated to VTU, Belagavi. The college offers 11 undergraduate and 6 postgraduate programs with 11 departments recognized as R&D centres by VTU, Belagavi, in addition to 5 QIP centres.

The core values of the institution are to be recognized as a premier technical institute committed to developing exemplary professionals, offering research-based innovative solutions and inspiring inventions for holistic socio­economic development.
